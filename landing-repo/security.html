Security & Permissions

Overview

SummarAI is designed with a "Privacy First" architecture. We understand that your meeting data is sensitive, and we have implemented strict controls to ensure your information remains secure.

Hardware Permissions

🎤 Microphone Access

Why? To record live meetings directly from your system.

Privacy: Audio is captured only when you explicitly click "Start Recording." Raw audio is streamed to our secure cloud processing pipeline.

🖥️ Screen/Window Capture

Why? To extract visual slides, whiteboard notes, and shared content during video meetings.

Privacy: The app takes periodic screenshots ONLY during active meeting sessions. Visual data is processed for OCR and discarded.

Data Processing

  • Cloud Processing: All data is encrypted during transit to our secure backend.
  • Storage: Session history is stored in an encrypted database.
  • No Model Training: We DO NOT use your data to train our AI models.
